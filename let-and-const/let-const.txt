What is the difference between var and let?
-Var you are able to redeclare and reassign a variable, with let you cannot redeclare it. Var is global scope, while let is block scope.
What is the difference between var and const?
-Var you are able to redelare and reassign, const you cannot do either. Const creates block scope.
What is the difference between let and const?
-With let you are able to reassign the value but you cannot with const
What is hoisting?
-Hoisting is where variables and function declarations are moved to to the top of their scope before execution. With hoisting, function declartions can be invoked before they are defined.
